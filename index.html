Key Ingredients in Every Chew

Prancy Pets Mobility Chews include 8 clinically proven and 100% safe ingredients that offer instant action and long-term benefits with regular use. Liver flavor turns our chews into tasty treats that your pets will love!

GLUCOSAMINE HCL

Helps keep the levels of synovial fluid at an optimal amount. This fluid keeps joints lubricated, reduces friction during movement, and fights soreness, both short & long term.

MSM

Has natural anti-inflammatory affects and helps support joint function, connective tissue health, and reduce pain & restore mobility.

CHONDROITIN

Provides extra joint protection, supports joint structure, and is an important building block for cartilage.

FISH & FLAXSEED OIL

Provides connective tissues with vital omega 3 & 6 fatty acids, which help build stronger joints, reduce stiffness, and encourage cartilage growth.
